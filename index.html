Scholarship Posts

Scholarshub is a website that is dedicated to serving top scholarship opportunities from around the world. We update the websites daily with top scholarship opportunities.

This project was developed to serve the needs of scholars, undergraduates and graduates that are seeking to further their education in top institutions in the world, and doing so at little or no cost.

Services Request

Through the services section of the website, scholars can request for an expert review of their Statement of Purpose and academic CV. This is geared towards ensuring that scholars put their best foot forward and enhance their chances of being selected.

Download Scholarship Resources

At Scholarshub, we also offer scholars the opportunity to download various resources that will aid their journey of securing a fully funded scholarship placement in top institutions around the world.

These resources are specially curated to meet the needs of scholars in the area of preparing for, and putting in a strong application.

About

I once spent months chasing one scholarship opportunity or the other so I understand how challenging it can be, especially when certain information are not readily available and when you have to deal with choosing which scholarship opportunity to focus on.

I remember having to learn of some scholarship opportunities just few days to their closing, and not having enough time to put in a befitting application.

This project was born out of my desire to make the scholarship search journey easier for scholars by not just providing timely, well-researched scholarship opportunities but also affording them the opportunity to gain access to quality materials that will make their application stand out.
